Welcome!

I’m a PhD student at Imperial College London working at the intersection of deep learning and immunology. My research focuses on reconstructing immune cell fate using single-cell transcriptomics, building predictive models for gene perturbations, and developing computer vision tools to quantify therapeutic responses in patient derived 3D models (organoids and spheroids).
